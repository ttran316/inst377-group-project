The Project

Problem

We are hoping to solve the problem of unprofessionalism in email of a corporate setting. We hope to have a translator of sorts that can produce effective terms when talking to coworkers or upper management.

Importance

Everything you say can determine your position in the company, fired or promoted. When that little bit of unprofessionalism slips through in your talk with coworkers, you might be in their sights for something unfavorable.

Solution

We have produced a translator, with the help of an external api, that could turn your simple words into professional jargon. Anyone you speak to will see your 5+ years of experience in the industry.
